# What Is Gebyar Matematika?

Gebyar Matematika is an annual mathematics competition that brings together students and math enthusiasts from across the region to showcase their mathematical prowess. It's an opportunity to celebrate the beauty of mathematics, challenge your problem-solving skills, and connect with fellow math enthusiasts.

Our website is your central hub for all things Gebyar Matematika. Here's what you can expect:

- Registration
  Ready to take on the challenge? Our website provides a simple and streamlined registration process. Just follow the link to register your team and secure your spot in the competition. Don't miss out on the chance to prove your mathematical mettle!

- Competition Details
  Want to know what's at stake? Explore the competition details section for information on categories, rules, and prizes. Whether you're a novice or an experienced mathematician, there's a category for everyone.
